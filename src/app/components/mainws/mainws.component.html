DID YOU KNOW

Most coffee including organic coffee is roasted in drum roasters which uses bottled (propane) or natural gas. When this gas mixes with an organic compound like a coffee bean it creates a poisonous gas called Hydrogen Sulfide (H2S) which then gets infused into the bean. BUT, are these beans still organic?” The answer is NO as they have now added a toxic chemical to the bean. Our roaster is 100% electric, using just a heating element and hot air to roast our organic beans. Our roaster also has Zero emissions with a neutral carbon footprint.
