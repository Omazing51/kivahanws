OUR STORY

KIVAHAN is a family business serving Specialty Organic Coffee. The Turkish background of one of the founders lead to the name KivaHan after the world’s first coffee shop that opened in Constantinople (Istanbul), in Turkey 1475. Coffee was of such great importance then, Turkish law deemed it legal for a woman to divorce her husband if he failed to provide her with her daily cup! As a roaster we search the world over to establish relationships with great farmers cultivating great coffees, and we roast them with extreme care.
